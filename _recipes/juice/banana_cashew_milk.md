title: Banana-Cashew Milk
author: jsonin
category: juice
yield: 1 bottle
---
* 1 cup raw cashews
* 1 banana
* 1 cup water
* pinch of cinnamon
* pinch of cardamom
* pinch of nutmeg

Soak the cashews in water for 2 hours. After the cashews have soacked, throw all the ingredients into a blender and puree for 1 minute.
